About the University

Excellence, mentoring, and productive citizenship.

Southwestern University Nigeria is described on its e-learning portal as an internationally recognized, intellectually vibrant institution that is accessible, community centered, technologically advanced, and responsive to diverse educational needs.

The university is devoted to excellence in teaching, learning, and research, with a focus on developing leaders who make a difference globally.

Admissions

Application flow

01

Create portal login

Go to the admission portal and create an account with name, email, and phone number.

02

Choose campus

After login, select Conversion, Ikeja, or Main campus and complete the application details.

03

Pay application fee

Submit the online form and pay through card, USSD, or bank transfer.

04

Check admission status

Log back in after 24 hours to check status, pay acceptance fees, and print the admission letter.
